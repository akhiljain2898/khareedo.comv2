Refund Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Summary: We operate a blanket manual refund policy. If you have any challenge at all (technical failure, poor results, or anything else). Email us with your Order ID and we will make it right. No refunds after the Excel file has been successfully downloaded.

1. Pricing

vendordhundo.com charges a flat fee of ₹99 per search, inclusive of all charges. You pay before the search runs. Payment is processed securely by Razorpay.

2. Refund Eligibility

Situation Refund
Technical error on our end. Pipeline failed or returned no results Yes, full refund
Payment charged but download link did not appear Yes, full refund
Results were poor quality or not useful for your query Yes, email us to explain
Excel file was successfully downloaded No refund
You changed your mind after payment No refund
Download link expired (30-minute window) No refund. Contact us to retrieve

3. How to Request a Refund

Email us at admin@verifiedwork.co with:

  • Your Order ID (shown on the result screen)
  • A brief description of what went wrong

We will review your request and respond within 72 hours. Approved refunds are processed back to the original payment method within 5–7 business days, subject to Razorpay's processing timelines.

4. Duplicate Payments

If you were charged more than once for the same search due to a technical error, the duplicate charge will be refunded in full. Please contact us with both Razorpay payment IDs.

5. No Cancellation After Download

vendordhundo.com delivers a digital file that is immediately accessible upon download. The service is considered fully delivered once the Excel file has been successfully downloaded. No cancellations or refunds are possible after this point.
